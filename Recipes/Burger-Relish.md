# Burger Relish

## Ingredients
- Olive oil
- Garlic cloves
- Chopped pomodorino tomatoes
- Finely chopped red onion
- Chopped Gherkin
- Salt, Pepper & Paprika
- Soy sauce

## Directions
1. Add all of the ingredients to a saucepan and simmer for 15 minutes on a low heat.
